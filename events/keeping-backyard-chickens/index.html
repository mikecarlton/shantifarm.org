Keeping Backyard Chickens

Pam Carlton March 2, 2019

Date/Time
Date(s) - 03/02/2019
11:00 am - 12:30 pm

Location
Shanti Farm

Categories

  • Animals

Have you ever dreamed of having chickens in your backyard?  When you want an omelet for breakfast you can just walk outside and collect the eggs from your own coop. During this fun, interactive and informative class you will learn all of the ins and outs of:

• Raising chicks
• Keeping chickens
• Finding or building the right coop for you
• Chicken nutrition
• Chicken health
• and more

Cost: $30/person
This class is limited to 10 participants so everyone gets a hands on experience.  A minimum of 4 participants is needed for the class to run.
