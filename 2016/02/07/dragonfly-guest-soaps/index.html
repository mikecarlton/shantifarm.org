Dragonfly Guest Soaps

Pam Carlton February 7, 2016 Soap

Yesterday my friend Stef, her daughter Sophia, my son Alex and I had a blast making soap.  Here is one of the dragonfly guest soaps we made with my new mold.  The kids chose the scent.  It is lemongrass with a little grapefruit.  The kids did great and the scent is AMAZING!  We put some flecks of turmeric in it for a little color.  These will be for sale after they cure (in 4 weeks).
